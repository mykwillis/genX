genX Overview

genX enables you to build genetic algorithm-based solutions for the Microsoft .NET platform.  Inspired by the natural processes of evolution, genetic algorithms excel at quickly finding good solutions to complex problems.  By providing efficient implementations of the most powerful variations of genetic algorithms, genX can put the power of genetic algorithms to work on your complex problem, whether it be in optimization, economics, machine learning, or any of countless other domains.

Using genX in your application is as simple as dropping the genX Genetic Algorithm Component onto the design surface in Microsoft Visual Studio .NET, setting a few properties in the Property window, and implementing a single event handler.  If you crave more complete control over the process, the extensive genX class library lets you customize and extend virtually any aspect of the algorithm.

This documentation provides an overview of genetic algorithms, their history, and some of their applications.  It then demonstrates how to use genX with a number of sample applications.

It is our hope that you will find genX a valuable tool for solving problems you have today, while also serving as an inspiration for finding new applications for genetic algorithms in the future.  Despite the attention they have been paid in academic circles over the past years, many theoretical discoveries pertaining to genetic algorithms have yet to find their way into mainstream computing, and many exciting uses are yet to be discovered.
