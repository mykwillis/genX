Supported Genetic Operators

Genetic Algorithms Implemented

  • Simple GA with non-overlapping populations.

Chromosome Representation

  • Fixed-length chromosomes with integer, double, binary, or custom gene type.

Mutation Operators

  • Random
  • Bitwise
  • Boundary

Recombination Operators

  • Single-point crossover
  • 2-point crossover
  • Uniform crossover
  • Partially Matched Crossover (PMX)

Reordering Operators

  • Swap
  • Inversion

Fitness Scaling

  • Proportional
  • Linear
  • Linear Ranked
  • Power Law

Selection

  • Roulette
  • Tournament
  • Stochastic Remainder Without Replacement
