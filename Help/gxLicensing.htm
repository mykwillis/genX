genX Developer's Guide  

Pricing and Licensing

genX is licensed on a per-developer basis.  Each developer working on a project that uses genX must have a valid license.

Evaluation

Evaluation versions of genX are available, which provide full functionality of the product.  These evaluation versions may be used for 15 days, after which they must be registered or removed from the system.  The evaluation version of genX may not be used in any production application.

Pricing and Licensing

Licenses must be purchased for each developer working on a project that uses the genX control.  A project with 10 people, but where only one developer uses genX at development time, requires 10 licenses.  This reflects the fact that the product as a whole is benefiting from the use of genX.

Retail pricing for a genX developer license can be found on our website at http://www.wanderlust-software.com .  Volume discounts for 10 or more licenses are available, as is special pricing for classroom use in schools.  genX is generally sold through resellers, who have some flexibility in pricing.

Upgrade Policy

We may occasionally release maintenance releases that contain bugfixes, optimizations, or other small changes. These releases may be downloaded at no charge by registered users of the same major version of that product for as long as the product is supported.

When a new major version of a product is released, registered users of the previous version may download the new version at no charge if their purchase was made within 30 days of the new release.  Significant discounts are available for upgrades from versions purchased more than 30 days before the new release. 

Tech Support

We will happily provide unlimited email tech support to registered users for one year from the purchase date, as well as to users in the 15 day evaluation period.  Send mail to support@wanderlust-software.com.
