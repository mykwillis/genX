Understanding Genetic Algorithms

Although it is possible to use genX in your application without really knowing what's going on under the hood, understanding some theory of genetic algorithms can help you understand the results you get from genX, and provide you the necessary background for tweaking the algorithm for better results on your problem.

The topics in this section explain the concepts behind genetic algorithms, and how those concepts are supported in genX.
